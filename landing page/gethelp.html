WHY DO I HAVE TO VERIFY MY PASSPORT INFORMATION?


As your Nike order is dispatched from abroad, local customs regulations require verification of your national ID, passport or delivery information to clear and deliver your order. Don't worry—we make clearing your order easy.

Your carrier for your order will email a secure link so you can confirm your Know Your Consumer (KYC) information. After you confirm your information, the carrier will clear and deliver your order. Delivery delays may result if you don't confirm your information within 30 days of receiving the carrier's first email. To ensure prompt delivery, the carrier may send you text (SMS) and email reminders or call you to confirm your information.

FAQs

What documents do I need to provide to confirm my KYC information?

Indian citizens can use either one of the documents below for verification:
  • Aadhaar
  • Voter ID
  • Passport
  • PAN card
When uploading a copy of your Aadhaar card, Voter ID or passport, remember to submit copies of the front and back. If you're using your passport for verification, ensure that it's still valid as expired passports are not accepted. If you're using your PAN card, you'll also need to submit a separate proof of address.

Please be aware that regulations require the address on your documents to match your order's delivery address. Inconsistent information can cause a potential delay in processing your package at customs.

I'm a foreign national living in India—how can I can confirm my KYC information?

A passport, visa or Persons of Indian Origin (PIO) card can be used to confirm your information. Additionally, customs will require proof of your address: a hotel booking receipt, stay visa, rental agreement, or you may provide proof of living with a relative or friend to confirm your address.

What if the address on my documents and the delivery address don't match?

If the address on your identification document doesn't match the delivery address, your order may be Returned to Sender (RTS). To avoid this, you must submit one of the following proof-of-address documents together with your identification document:
  • Bank account statement
  • Electricity
  • Hotel booking receipt
  • LPG connection receipt
  • Rent agreement
  • Hostel ID card/university ID with hostel fee receipt
  • Driving licence
If you have stated your workplace as the order's delivery address, please submit an Employee ID copy that includes your office address.

For all of the above, please ensure that the address stated on the proof-of-address document matches the delivery address on the order to avoid processing delays at customs.

I didn't submit the correct documents—what should I do?

Don't worry, the carrier will contact you directly if you submitted documents not listed above, or if they need additional documentation to verify your information and clear your order through customs.

Do I have to verify my KYC information every time I place an order?

No, once you place an order, the carrier will keep your information on file to clear future orders automatically. You won't be contacted again to confirm your information unless you have changed your legal name or address since your last KYC verification.

I've already verified my KYC information—why is the carrier asking again?

You'll be contacted by the carrier again only if you placed an order that has a different name, delivery address, email or phone number than what the carrier has on file from a previous order.
